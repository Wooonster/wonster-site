# Design Context

## Product
whatsmy.fun — a personal research site with a wiki, daily paper recommendations, and blog. Built by a researcher who shares ML/AI notes publicly.

## Audience
Researchers and the general public. People who care about ideas, read papers, follow ML discourse. They're comfortable with density but appreciate clarity.

## Tone
Precise, playful, editorial. Like a well-designed research journal that occasionally winks. Not sterile, not loud. Think: Quanta Magazine meets a personal zine.

## Theme
Light default. Warm off-whites, ink-black text, deliberate accent colors. No dark-mode-first aesthetics.

## What to avoid
- Generic AI aesthetics (cyan-on-dark, glowing purple gradients)
- Cards inside cards
- Same-size card grids
- Pure black/white — always tint
- Overused fonts (Inter, Roboto, system defaults)
